Project Overview

From Hours of Manual Work to Full Automation: Managing customers, opportunities, agreements, deposits, invoices, and sales orders in NetSuite was a tedious, error-prone process, consuming 30–50 hours each month. Delays, mistakes, and inefficiencies slowed down operations.

By integrating NetSuite into a utility portal, we fully automated these tasks, reducing manual work to zero. Accuracy improved, efficiency soared, and scalability became effortless. Now, teams focus on growth instead of repetitive admin work, turning a major bottleneck into a seamless, streamlined process.

The Challenge

Managing key business operations in NetSuite—such as creating customers, opportunities, agreements, deposits, invoices, and sales orders—was entirely manual. This process was not only time-consuming but also prone to human errors, leading to inefficiencies, delays, and inconsistencies in data management. Employees spent 30–50 hours per month on repetitive tasks, diverting valuable time from strategic initiatives. As the workload grew, scalability became a major concern, making it clear that a more efficient solution was needed.
