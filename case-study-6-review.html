The Challenge

North Carolina Railroad was struggling with inefficient manual processes that consumed valuable employee time and introduced significant operational risks. The transportation industry demands precision, speed, and reliability, but their legacy processes were falling short.

Time-Intensive Manual Work

Employees spent 30-50 hours monthly on repetitive data entry tasks across customers, opportunities, agreements, deposits, invoices, and sales orders.

High Error Rates

Manual processes led to data inconsistencies, delayed transactions, and costly mistakes that impacted customer satisfaction and regulatory compliance.

Resource Drain

Skilled personnel were bogged down with administrative tasks instead of focusing on strategic initiatives and customer service excellence.

Scalability Issues

As business grew, manual processes couldn't keep pace, creating bottlenecks that limited expansion and market responsiveness.

Business Impact Before Automation:

  • $15,000+ monthly in labor costs for manual processing
  • 25% of working hours dedicated to administrative tasks
  • 15-20% error rate in data entry and processing
  • 48-hour average processing time for complex transactions
  • Limited scalability hindering business growth

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive automation solution that seamlessly integrated NetSuite ERP with a custom utility portal, transforming manual processes into fully automated workflows. This enterprise-grade solution eliminated human intervention while maintaining complete data integrity and compliance.

Technical Architecture

NetSuite ERP Integration

Direct API integration with NetSuite's SuiteScript and REST APIs for real-time data synchronization.

Node.js Backend

Robust server-side processing with Express.js framework and automated job scheduling.

Enterprise Security

OAuth 2.0 authentication, data encryption, and audit trails for compliance and security.
