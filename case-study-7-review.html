The Challenge

North Carolina Railroad faced significant challenges in managing complex customer relationships as their utility portal expanded. The traditional CRM approach couldn't handle the sophisticated relationship structures required for utility services.

Multi-Company Associations

Single individuals associated with multiple companies required duplicate contact records, leading to data inconsistency and management complexity.

Data Duplication

Separate records for each company association created redundant data, increasing maintenance overhead and error rates.

Relationship Tracking

Difficulty in tracking complete customer relationships across multiple business entities and service interactions.

Scalability Issues

Growing client base overwhelmed the existing system, leading to performance degradation and operational bottlenecks.

Business Impact Before CRM Enhancement:

  • 60% of contacts had duplicate records
  • $200,000+ annual cost in data management
  • 40% increase in administrative workload
  • 25% error rate in customer communications
  • Limited scalability for business growth

The Solution

We developed an advanced CRM system that seamlessly integrates with NetSuite CRM, providing sophisticated multi-company relationship management capabilities. This enterprise-grade solution transformed how North Carolina Railroad manages complex customer relationships.
