The Challenge

The client was struggling with a highly fragmented manufacturing ecosystem that lacked end-to-end visibility. Production workflows, label generation, component verification, and activation tracking were handled in siloed processes across multiple teams.

Poor Visibility

Production and distribution data were scattered across systems, preventing real-time decision-making.

Defect Risk

Defective or unauthorized components were not being flagged quickly enough, creating quality and compliance issues.

Manual Activation

Activation and licensing workflows required manual intervention, slowing time to market.

Distribution Complexity

Coordinating freight, warehouse, and carrier operations was inefficient and error-prone.

Business Impact Before Transformation:

  • 35% increase in product delays due to poor workflow visibility
  • 20% defect rate before root cause registration
  • 24 hours average time to identify production exceptions
  • Multiple manual approvals across more than 5 teams

The Solution

Kyra Technologies delivered a unified manufacturing production system built on NetSuite and MPS. The solution provided real-time process orchestration, component tracking, licensing workflows, and production activation control.

Technical Architecture

Production Orchestration

Managed complex manufacturing sequences, component approvals, and activation checkpoints.

NetSuite Integration

Connected ERP, inventory, and order data for seamless manufacturing and distribution workflows.

Secure Data Flow

Encrypted transactional data and audit trails ensured compliance across the supply chain.
