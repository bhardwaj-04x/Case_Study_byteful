The Challenge

North Carolina Railroad faced critical operational inefficiencies that were severely impacting their utility occupancy application process. The traditional paper-based system created significant delays, cost overruns, and frustration for both applicants and internal staff.

Year-Long Processing Times

Applications took up to 12 months to complete, causing significant delays in infrastructure projects and economic development initiatives.

Manual Paperwork Burden

Extensive paperwork requirements, manual reviews, and physical document handling created bottlenecks and increased error rates.

Poor Communication

Lack of real-time communication between applicants and reviewers led to misunderstandings and unnecessary delays.

Economic Development Impact

Delayed approvals hindered business growth, job creation, and infrastructure development in the region.

Business Impact Before Digital Transformation:

  • 12 months average processing time per application
  • $500,000+ annual cost in manual processing
  • 40% error rate in paperwork and documentation
  • 200+ hours per application in administrative work
  • Delayed economic development affecting regional growth

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive digital portal that revolutionized the utility occupancy application process. This enterprise-grade solution combined modern web technologies with seamless integrations to create a streamlined, user-friendly experience for all stakeholders.

Technical Architecture

Node.js Backend

Scalable server-side processing with RESTful APIs and real-time WebSocket communication.

MongoDB Database

Flexible document-based storage for complex application data and file attachments.

DocuSign Integration

Secure electronic signature capabilities for legal document processing.
